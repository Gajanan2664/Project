Mini Project on Design and Fabrication of Mini Hand Saw Machine


The effective design of mini hand saw machine is to minimize the time taken for removing or cutting a wood material. hand saws, also known as "panel saws", are used to cut pieces of wood into different shapes. This is usually done in order to join the pieces together and carve a wooden object. They usually operate by having a series of sharp points of some substance that is harder than the wood being cut. The hand saw is a bit like a tenon saw, but with one flat, sharp edge
